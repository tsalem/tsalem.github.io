    About Me

    I am a Clinical Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer and Information Technology at Purdue University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Kentucky under the direction of Dr. Nathan Jacobs. My research focuses on Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Computing Education. I design and develop computational models and systems for analyzing visual and textual data to enhance our understanding of the world. Additionally, I am actively working on advancing computer science education by integrating machine learning and AI into innovative online platforms. My teaching experience includes various Computer Science and Information Technology courses, covering topics such as computer programming, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
